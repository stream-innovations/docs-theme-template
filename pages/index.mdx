Introducing

StreamPay™ is a groundbreaking mobile application and wallet that leverages Solana web3, blockchain technologies, and AI to redefine payment solutions. It focuses on frictionless, contactless, biometric, and cross-border payments, offering a secure and seamless payment experience for users and merchants worldwide. With advanced technology at its core, StreamPay™ aims to revolutionize transactions by providing enhanced convenience, security, and efficiency.
